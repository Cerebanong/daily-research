## Topic Coverage Gaps

1. **Databricks — Security and Compliance:** The Lakewatch SIEM launch and growing enterprise compliance features (HIPAA, ABAC, governed tags) represent a strategic product expansion that current search queries do not specifically target.

2. **AI Innovation — Chinese AI Labs:** DeepSeek V4, Qwen 3.6-Plus, and Moonshot AI's Kimi Code all appeared in findings via secondary sources. No Chinese AI lab blogs are in the direct source list, creating a coverage gap for the second-largest AI ecosystem.

3. **AI Governance — U.S. State Legislation:** Multiple states passed significant AI bills in the review period (Washington, Texas, Oklahoma), but "state AI legislation" is not a search query. The volume of state-level activity warrants dedicated tracking.

4. **Data Governance — Financial Services Regulation:** DORA enforcement, BCBS 239 compliance, and SEC examination priorities all appeared in findings, but coverage was incidental rather than systematic. The financial services data regulation queries could be broadened.

5. **Cross-Topic — Agentic AI Governance:** The intersection of agentic AI capabilities and governance requirements (McKinsey survey, SS&C framework, Palo Alto Networks AES) is an emerging theme not well captured by any single topic's current queries.
