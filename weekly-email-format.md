# Weekly Email Format

## Subject Line

### Monday–Thursday (Single Topic Deep-Dive)
Weekly {Topic Name} Briefing — Week of {start date} to {end date}

### Friday (Full Roundup)
Weekly Research Roundup — Week of {start date} to {end date}

## Structure

### Opening
2–3 sentence executive summary of the week's key developments for the topic (Mon–Thu) or across all topics (Friday).

### Top Stories (up to 5)

For each story:

#### {Story Number}. {Headline}

{3–5 sentence summary providing context, significance, and implications.}

**Source:** [Source Name](URL) — Published {Date}
**Why it matters:** One sentence on relevance to data and AI professionals.

### Notable Mentions
Brief bullets (1 sentence each) for stories that didn't make the top 5 but are worth tracking. Include source citation.

### Closing
"This briefing covers {topic/all topics} for the week of {dates}. Sources and methodology details are maintained in the daily-research repository."

## Tone
Professional, suitable for LinkedIn sharing and forwarding to colleagues. Analytical rather than promotional. Proper source attribution throughout.

## Format
HTML email. Clean typography with headers, bold text, numbered stories, and hyperlinked sources. Keep total length under 1,500 words.

## Citation Standard
Every claim, statistic, or announcement must link to its original source. Format: [Source Name](URL). This content may be shared on LinkedIn and other publications — proper attribution is required.
